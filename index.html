About World Coding Day

World Coding Day is an annual global celebration of coding and digital literacy that takes place every October 10th. It was established to promote the importance of coding skills in the modern world and to encourage people of all ages to explore the world of programming.

Mission

Democratize access to coding education worldwide

Foster innovation and creative problem-solving

Build a supportive global coding community

Prepare the next generation for digital careers

Key Events Timeline

2015

First World Coding Day launched with 50 events in 30 countries

2018

Reached 1 million participants worldwide

2020

Pivoted to virtual events during pandemic, expanding reach

2023

Record participation with over 5 million coders across 180 countries
